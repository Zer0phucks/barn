What BARN Does

We connect vacant and neglected properties with families experiencing housing insecurity. Property owners grant us the right to use their properties, and we place vetted caretakers who maintain the home in exchange for stable housing. Neighborhoods are revitalized, properties stay maintained, and families get a foundation to thrive.
